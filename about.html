about me

I am a mechanical engineer by trade, born and raised in Seattle, Washington. I have a lots of experience and familiarity utilizing CAD tools, engineering fundamentals and mechanical design.
I'm driven by a passion for problem-solving, a constant desire to learn how the world works, and a focus on self-improvement and continual development of new skills. Spending lots of my personal time on creative projects, I have some largely self-taught experience in woodworking, 3D printing, rendering, programming, and a myriad of other skills I've picked up along the way.
I also love exploring the outdoors and staying active; some of my favorite activites are hiking and backpacking, playing soccer and tennis, and skiing.

I've recently relocated to Los Angeles, and am searching for exciting opportunities in the LA area!
